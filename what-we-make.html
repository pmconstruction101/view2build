02What we make

Made for your space.

Three things, done properly: kitchens, fitted bedroom furniture, and built-in joinery. Every piece is configured to your layout from our standard cabinet sizes, machined to tolerance, and professionally fitted within our install area.

01KitchensDesigned · manufactured · installed

Kitchens, machined to the millimetre

From the first survey to the final handle, your kitchen is planned around how you actually live, built from our range of standard cabinet sizes, configured to your layout. Shaker or handleless, painted or veneered, drawn in 3D so you see it before a single sheet is cut, then CNC-machined for a fit that's exact, every time.

02Wardrobes & Fitted Bedroom FurnitureFloor-to-ceiling · exact-fit

Storage that uses every inch

Sliding-door wardrobes made to the exact dimensions of your room, into the eaves, around the chimney breast, along the awkward wall. Because the carcass sits behind the doors, the fit is wall-to-wall, floor-to-ceiling. Hinged-door wardrobes and fitted bedroom furniture use our standard cabinet sizes configured to your layout. Hand-finished, soft-close, and built so the lines run true from one end to the other.
