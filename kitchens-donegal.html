Bespoke kitchens & fitted joinery in Co. Donegal

View2Build is a Donegal maker — our workshop is in Pettigo, so kitchens for Donegal homes are made locally and installed by the same person who designed them. We work right across the county and throughout Ireland.

01In DonegalKitchens · Wardrobes · Joinery

Donegal's cottages, coastal homes, farmhouses and self-builds rarely suit a standard kitchen. We draw everything in 3D, machine it on an industrial CNC and fit it ourselves — bespoke kitchens, floor-to-ceiling wardrobes, media walls and fitted joinery made to your room.

Towns we regularly work in across County Donegal include Letterkenny, Ballybofey, Stranorlar, Donegal Town, Ballyshannon, Bundoran, Pettigo and Lifford.

Everything is drawn in 3D so you see it before it's built, cut on an industrial CNC for a precise, consistent finish, and fitted by the same person who designed it — no showroom, no middleman. We return a detailed quote within 3–5 working days.
